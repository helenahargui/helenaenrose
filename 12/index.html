Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need to regularly update my software and apps?

Regular updates fix security vulnerabilities, introduce new features, and improve system stability.

How does facial recognition work?

It uses algorithms to map facial features and compare them to a database, helping to identify a person.

Why is turning it off and on again a common tech fix?

Restarting can clear out faulty states, reset configurations, and free up system resources, often resolving issues.
